Hobbies

Knitting/crocheting

My hobbies include many kinds of crafts, a few of which being knitting and crocheting. It's actually kind of difficult to explain why I like such things so much - I suppose the ability to create something, particularly something useful, is somewhat satisfying, not to mention how satisfying it is to simply see your own handiwork and notice how much you've improved.

Following instructions is not particularly difficult for me, but I can't deny sometimes preferring to just wing it most of the time, testing and redoing projects until they turn out how I want them to.

Crafts

Aside from knitting and crocheting, I also enjoy all other sorts of crafts, such as sewing or paper crafts. Particularly, I love creating miniatures and small scale versions of everyday things, perhaps due to the convenience of being able to work on them easily in the small space of my room, as well as due to the amount of materials they need. It is certainly a lot cheaper to make a 1/12 scale dress than it is to make a full scale one.
